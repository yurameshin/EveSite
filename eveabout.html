Who is Eve?

Eve (born 23 May 1995), also formerly known as Keitora (けいとら) and Kurowa (クロわ), is a Japanese singer-songwriter and Vocaloid producer. He first debuted in the music industry by singing covers of popular music on Niconico, a apanese social media site.

Eve's Journey


2009

Eve began his career on NicoNico under the alias Kurowa, singing "TRAGIC BOY"

2012

Eve became the lead singer for the Japanese indie band Einie

2015

Eve debuted with his first album, "Wonder World"

2016

Eve release his third album "Official Number", which became his first-ever charting album.

2017

Eve released "Dramaturgy", a song that many people know him for. It still remains the second most popular song of his today.

2020

Eve released two albums, Otogi and Smile, alongside "KaiKai Kitan", which was the opening song for "Jujutsu Kaisen". This song skyrocketed his popularity and is his most played song today.

2022

Eve released "Fight Song", the 12th Ending Theme for the anime "Chainsaw Man"

TODAY

Eve's popularity still continues to increase exponentially, with him even having finished his first overseas tour, "Culture" recently.
